About this Data Dashboard

Overview

This data dashboard was created for Oregon Shores Conservation Coalition's CoastWatch Mile Analysis Project, with support from The Builder's Initiative. The purpose of this dashboard is to visualize data submitted by CoastWatch volunteers who complete mile reports for detailing observations along the Oregon Coast.

How to use this data dashboard
  1. Click on the interactive charts in the side panel to filter data shown in the charts.
  2. Click "Reset Filters" in the navigation pane to remove all selected filters from the charts.
  3. Click on the map layer icon in the upper-right corner of the map to toggle data displayed on the map.
  4. Click on features to view relevant data attributes.
  5. Click on a polygon feature to zoom the map to its extent.
Map layers

  • CoastWatch mile reports

    • Description: Community science observations by CoastWatch volunteers who have adopted a mile-long segment of the Oregon Coast
    • Source: Oregon Shores Conservation Coalition

  • Marine reserves

    • Description: Oregon’s marine reserves dedicated to conservation and scientific research
    • Source: Oregon Department of Fish & Wildlife

  • Rocky shores

    • Description: Rocky intertidal managed areas designated as either marine education areas, marine research areas, or marine conservation areas
    • Source: Oregon Department of Fish & Wildlife
